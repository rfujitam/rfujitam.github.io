Ryo Fujita / 藤田 遼

Research Institute for Mathematical Sciences (RIMS), Kyoto University, Assistant Professor

Email: rfujita (at) kurims.kyoto-u.ac.jp

Research Interests: Representation Theory, Quantum Algebra

I am working on representation theory of algebras related to complex semisimple Lie algebras or their root systems, with a particular focus on affine quantum groups.
